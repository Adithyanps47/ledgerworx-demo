The Process of Business Setup in Dubai

1

Find your Business Activity

Before you open a company in Dubai, research on feasibility and profitability to determine the nature of your venture.

2

Select the Jurisdiction

Choose between Mainland, Free Zone and Offshore jurisdictions based on your business requirements and regulations.

3

Choose a Company Name

Ensure your business name conforms to UAE's strict naming conventions and get approval from authorities.

4

Get Initial Approval

Submit an application for initial approval to the Dubai DED to ensure no objections to your business startup.

5

Open a Bank Account

Open a corporate bank account with local or international banks available in the UAE.

6

Rent an Office

All Dubai-based businesses require a physical address. Find suitable office space for your operations.

7

Apply for Final Approvals

Submit all documents, lease agreements, and service agent contracts for final government authorization.
