Frequently Asked Questions

How long does business setup take? ‚ñº
Most business setups take 5-7 business days. Complex structures or additional approvals may take longer. We'll provide a timeline during initial consultation.
What are the required documents for business setup? ‚ñº
Required documents typically include: Passport copies, Emirates ID (if applicable), business plan, bank statements, and proof of address. We'll provide a complete checklist based on your specific setup type.
What's the difference between Free Zone and Mainland setup? ‚ñº
Free Zones offer 100% foreign ownership, no local partner needed, and tax incentives. Mainland businesses can trade locally but require a local sponsor (51% ownership). We help you choose the best option for your business.
How much does business setup cost? ‚ñº
Costs vary based on business type and location. Our packages start from AED 2,500 for startup setups. Government fees and license costs are separate. We provide transparent pricing upfront.
Do I need a local sponsor? ‚ñº
Local sponsors are required for mainland businesses but not for Free Zone setups. We can assist with sponsor arrangements if needed.
Can I sponsor work visas for my employees? ‚ñº
Yes, once your company is registered, you can sponsor work visas for employees. The number of visas depends on your company structure and capital. We provide guidance throughout the process.
